How to remove the background of a picture.

1. Select.

For best results, choose an image where the subject has clear edges with nothing overlapping.

2. Remove.

Upload your image to automatically remove the background in an instant.

3. Continue editing.

Download your new image as a PNG file with a transparent background to save, share, or keep editing in Adobe Express.

Frequently asked questions.

What makes Adobe Express the best background remover?

Our background remover tool uses professional-quality and intuitive tools to remove backgrounds in seconds. Best of all, it’s free to use anytime, anywhere on both your desktop and mobile device.

Can I remove the background on my phone?

Absolutely. Quickly and easily remove the background from photos on your mobile device by launching the background remover, uploading an image, and downloading. If you are looking for an app to remove backgrounds with, consider downloading the Adobe Express app to remove backgrounds even more efficiently.

How do I make a background transparent?

Removing the background from your photo will automatically make the background transparent. Your new image will download as a PNG file, which supports transparency, so you can place your newly edited image on any background you like.

Can I change the photo’s background?

Yes, you can easily use this tool as a photo background changer. Once you have removed the background from a picture, change the background in your photo by opening the full Adobe Express editor. Add your image to a new project to place it on a background of your choice.

Can I use Adobe Express for free? If so, what’s included?

Yes, we have a free plan available for anyone. Enjoy everything you need to stand out, including dozens of Quick Action tools, thousands of templates, photo editing and effects, and much more. Visit our plans and pricing for details.
